Nutrition Support

Oneiro Pharmacy supplies enteral nutrients and feeding equipment for hospitals, long-term care communities, and home care patients. From standard polymeric formulas to specialty and disease-specific options, we keep your patients' nutrition therapy on schedule with reliable delivery and clinical support.

  • Standard, high-protein, and calorie-dense formulas
  • Specialty and disease-specific formulas (renal, diabetic, pulmonary)
  • Peptide-based and elemental formulas for malabsorption
  • Feeding pumps, bags, sets, and extension supplies
  • Adult and pediatric options with dietitian-friendly substitution guidance
  • Scheduled deliveries matched to your census and par levels
