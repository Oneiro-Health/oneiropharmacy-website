Parenteral Nutrition

Oneiro Pharmacy compounds patient-specific TPN formulations in our USP <797> compliant cleanrooms. Every bag is prepared to your prescriber's exact specifications and verified by our pharmacists before release, so your facility receives safe, accurate parenteral nutrition on a dependable schedule.

  • Fully customized adult, pediatric, and neonatal TPN formulations
  • 2-in-1 and 3-in-1 (total nutrient admixture) preparations
  • Electrolyte, vitamin, and trace element adjustments per patient labs
  • Daily and cyclic delivery schedules to match your census
  • STAT compounding for urgent new starts and formula changes
  • 24/7 pharmacist consultation for your clinical team
