Pediatric Nutrition

We supply infant formulas for newborn nurseries, NICUs, pediatric units, and discharge programs — including standard, specialty, and hypoallergenic options. Our pharmacists work with your clinical team to keep the right formula in stock and on time.

  • Standard milk-based and soy-based infant formulas
  • Hypoallergenic and extensively hydrolyzed formulas
  • Amino acid-based formulas for severe allergies
  • Preterm and high-calorie formulas for NICU patients
  • Metabolic and specialty formulas by prescription
  • Reliable supply for facilities and take-home programs
